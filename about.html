About the Project

The University Housing Complex Maintenance System (UHCMS) is a centralized web-based platform designed to simplify and accelerate the maintenance request process for university residents.


By providing an intuitive digital portal, UHCMS ensures that residents can easily log issues such as electrical failures, plumbing leaks, or carpentry needs directly into the administrative queue. This prevents communication delays, ensures accurate record-keeping, and helps our maintenance staff resolve problems efficiently to ensure a comfortable living environment for everyone.
